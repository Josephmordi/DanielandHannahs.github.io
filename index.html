MISSION SAFE HOUSE PROJECT

MISSION SAFE HOUSE PROJECT(MSHP) had been a dream or vision ,

We ran from it many years ago, over 11 years now because we didn't have a stable job and had no money as it were to fund it, the more we ran the more God move us closer everyday to it.

We finally began to respond at last only 2years ago, we were fortunate to work with a group of brothers who picked up a rented apartment in ASABA for a project, A security and governance work we did together for the British council, our project lasted for 2years, all the time I was the one who stayed in ASABA facility,

I began looking at the three/four bedroom flat, it Began looking at me, I knew I could no longer run.. Once the project ended, I turned or converted the facility to what you now see today

When the project was over, I started counselling and mentoring a bunch of youths, we called them next generation spiritual leadership and fixing impending gap we foresee, our life has never been the same!

I thought it was just a one off encounter with a few youths, but we have mentored over 35 youths, ignited their life with fire to follow on in this way, preparing them for missions, leadership, character, spiritual service in local assemblies.
